Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2026

Snake Rush ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

If you sign in using Firebase Authentication, we may collect your email address and a unique user ID provided by Google Firebase.

Game Data

We store gameplay progress, coins, upgrades, and related game data securely using Firebase Firestore.

Purchase Information

If you make in-app purchases, transactions are processed securely by Google Play Billing. We do not store your payment information.

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain game functionality
  • Save and sync game progress
  • Process in-app purchases
  • Improve game performance and user experience

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information.

We may share limited information with trusted third-party services such as:

  • Google Firebase
  • Google Play Services

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

5. Children's Privacy

Snake Rush does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us for removal.
