Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 7, 2026

Thank you for using PeekMaster. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By accessing or using PeekMaster, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by all the terms of this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: When you create a profile, you may provide a username. We do not require email or phone number registration.
  • Game Data: We store your game progress, scores, achievements, and statistics locally on your device.
  • Purchase Information: If you make in-app purchases, transaction data is processed by the Google Play Store and may include purchase history.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and app version for troubleshooting and analytics.
  • Usage Data: Game session data, play time, feature usage, and performance metrics to improve user experience.
  • Crash Reports: Anonymous crash logs to help us identify and fix bugs.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our game services
  • To track your game progress and calculate vision scores
  • To unlock achievements and reward milestones
  • To process in-app purchases through Google Play
  • To detect and prevent technical issues or fraud
  • To analyze app performance and improve user experience
  • To send you service-related notifications (optional)

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage

Your game data, statistics, and preferences are stored locally on your device using encrypted DataStore. This data remains under your control and can be deleted by uninstalling the app.

3.2 Cloud Sync (Future Feature)

If cloud sync is introduced in future updates, you will be prompted to opt-in and your data will be stored securely on our servers.

3.3 Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 Google Play Services

PeekMaster uses the following Google services:

  • Google Play Billing: For processing in-app purchases. All payment information is handled by Google Play. We do not store your credit card or payment details.
  • Google Play Services: For app authentication and integrity checks.

4.2 Analytics

We may use anonymous analytics to understand how users interact with our app. This data is aggregated and does not personally identify you.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information in the following circumstances:

  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or governmental authority.
  • Protection of Rights: To protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property, or that of our users.
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets.

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Data Access and Control

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data stored locally on your device
  • Delete your local game data (uninstall the app or use in-app delete option)
  • Opt out of analytics collection (if available in settings)

6.2 In-App Purchases

All purchases are processed through Google Play. To request refunds or manage subscriptions, please use Google Play's refund and subscription management tools.

PeekMaster is not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal data from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete that information promptly.
