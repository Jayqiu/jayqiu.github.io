Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 30 August 2024

Thank you for using HowFast Schulte Table ("the App"). Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

1. About the App

HowFast SchulteTable is a Schulte Table app designed to help users improve their cognitive abilities through gameplay. The app offers features such as game play, viewing history, and customizing settings like light/dark mode, grid size, and table type (numbers/letters). The App does not collect any personal data from users.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the data necessary to enhance user experience and ensure the app's functionality. Specifically, we collect:

  • App Interaction Data: Data related to user interactions within the app, such as gameplay records, settings changes, and other non-personal user preferences.
  • Crash Reports: Data collected through Firebase Crashlytics to help us identify and fix technical issues.
  • Analytics Data: Data gathered through Firebase Analytics to understand how users engage with the app, including screen views, in-app events, and other usage patterns.
  • Push Notifications: We may send push notifications to inform users about updates, new features, or other relevant information. Users can control push notifications through their device settings.

3. Why We Collect Data

The data we collect is used solely to improve the performance and user experience of the app, identify and fix issues, and analyze usage patterns. We do not collect personal data or share any data with third parties, except as necessary for the operation of the app (e.g., Firebase services).

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the data we collect from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. Since no personal data is collected, there is no need for data deletion requests.

5. User Rights and Compliance

As we do not collect personal data, user rights concerning personal data, such as the right to access or delete data, are not applicable. However, we are committed to complying with relevant data protection laws and regulations to ensure the highest level of security and privacy for our users.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. Please review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.
